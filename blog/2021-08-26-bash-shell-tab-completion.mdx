Batect now supports shell tab completion in Bash, in addition to the [existing support for Fish and Zsh](/docs/getting-started/shell-tab-completion).

This supports both options (eg. `./batect --out`<kbd>Tab</kbd> completes to `./batect --output`) and task names
(eg. `./batect b`<kbd>Tab</kbd> completes to `./batect build`).

To get started, update your project to Batect 0.73.0, then follow
[the installation instructions](/docs/getting-started/shell-tab-completion#bash).
